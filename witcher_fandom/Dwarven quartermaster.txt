Dwarven quartermaster
The quartermaster is a dwarf who is part of Merton Bringgs' band of mercenaries. They have been hired by Merwin and Sabrina to track and find Deidre, Merwin's sister.
He can be found at the more southerly end of the two campfires north and west of the Camp. He sells a variety of goods, including:
flint
goose fat
blade enhancements: whetstones, grindstones, diamond dust
torch
heavy weapons: Temerian steel sword, axe
short weapons: Temerian iron and steel daggers
