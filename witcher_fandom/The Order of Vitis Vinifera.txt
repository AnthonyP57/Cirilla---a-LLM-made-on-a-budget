The Order of Vitis Vinifera
The Order of Vitis Vinifera is a quest item in the Blood and Wine expansion, which is considered the highest honor in Toussaint.
It is rewarded to Geralt by Anna Henrietta, if he chose to kill Dettlaff and save Syanna during the main quest, Tesham Mutna.
Associated quest
Pomp and Strange Circumstance
Trivia
Vitis Vinifera is the scientific name for the common grape vine.
