Castle Barienmurg
Castle of Barienmurg is the largest castle in the Northern Kingdoms, which was built by the Order of the Flaming Rose, a religious knightly order of the Eternal Fire in Redania, during the leadership of Grand Master Siegfried of Denesle.
Trivia
History, connection to religion and also name is reference to Polish castle Malbork (Marienburg).
Murg means monk or priest in language of Albania. There is also river by the same name in Baden-Württemberg, a state of Germany.
