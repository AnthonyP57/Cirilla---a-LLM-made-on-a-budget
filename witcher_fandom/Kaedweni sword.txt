Kaedweni sword
Kaedweni sword is a steel sword in The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings. It is not to be confused with the identically named, yet slightly more powerful Kaedweni sword crafted using Diagram: Robust Kaedweni sword.
Geralt can loot this version of the Kaedweni sword from bodies of Kaedweni soldiers in Chapter 2.
