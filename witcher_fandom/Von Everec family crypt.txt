Von Everec family crypt
Von Everec family crypt is located near their manor. The mausoleum was built as quite a work of art in family's golden age and as such is even described in Famous Redanian Dynasties tome. As name suggests, its final resting place of many von Everecs including Vlodimir, Olgierd's brother and second-in-command of Redanian Free Company.
Associated quest
Dead Man's Party
Notes
After Dead Man's Party is completed, one can return here to find a columbine flower has been left at Vlodimir's grave, most likely from Shani.
