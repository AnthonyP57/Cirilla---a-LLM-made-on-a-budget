Nadia Esposito
Nadia Esposito was a racist Rivian young noblewoman and a war orphan. She is generally considered to have been the spark which sent a furious crowd into the streets, the infamous Rivian Pogrom.
Biography
In 1268, in the marketplace of the city of Rivia, a dwarf merchant importuned her. When Nadia called her friends for avenge the slight, even the dwarf called his mates. The thing involved immediately in a carnage since to the clash they joined all the Rivian citizens that for a long time harbored prejudices against the nonhumans. 184 between humans, dwarves, elves and gnomes perished.
