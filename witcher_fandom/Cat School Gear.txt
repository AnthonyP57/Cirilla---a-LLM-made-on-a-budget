Cat School Gear
Cat or Feline School Gear is a witcher armor and swords set inspired by the School of the Cat for witchers and is one of the Scavenger Hunts in The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt. The set includes chest armor, steel and silver swords, crossbow, gauntlets, boots, and trousers, all of which are upgradeable except the crossbow. Also, there is not a New Game + version of the crossbow.
The armor set is worn by Gaetan in the DLC secondary quest Where the Cat and Wolf Play..., as well as presumably being worn by Kiyan before he was experimented on by a mage. According to said mage's notes, the gear used by witchers of the School of the Cat is heavily inspired by elven weapons and armor.
Bonuses for 3 pieces: Strong Attack increase Fast Attack damage for 5 seconds by 10% for each piece of the set.
Bonuses for 6 pieces: Rear attacks deal 50% more damage, and also stun the opponent at the cost of 1 Adrenaline Point(s).
Note: all legendary gear are stronger versions of base game gear in New Game +.
