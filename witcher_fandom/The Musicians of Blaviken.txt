The Musicians of Blaviken
The Musicians of Blaviken are fairytale beings in the Land of a Thousand Fables illusion. They comprise of a rooster standing on a cat that stands on a dog who is standing on a donkey.
Associated quest
Beyond Hill and Dale...
Trivia
They are based on Town Musicians of Bremen by Brothers Grimm.
Notes
If you try to get too close to them, they'll poof and disappear, leading to Syanna making a comment.
