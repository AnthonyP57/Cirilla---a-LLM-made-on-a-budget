Barghests
Barghests is a book in The Witcher. A bestiary volume on barghests, based on the accounts of those who have witnessed their attacks.
Content
Barghests
The text consists of statements by people who have seen barghest attacks. These eyewitness accounts provide a lot of valuable information.
