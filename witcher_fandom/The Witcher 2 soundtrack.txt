The Witcher 2 soundtrack
 The soundtrack for The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings was composed by Adam Skorupa and Krzysztof Wierzynkiewicz. The soundtrack CD is included with the game and consists of 23 tracks with a total running time of approximately 73 minutes and 1 second. Four additional tracks were released through GOG.com.
The 43-track version is also available for free download on the CD PROJEKT RED website.
