Vampire: Bruxa (gwent card)
Vampire: Bruxa is a close combat gwent card in The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt and part of the Monsters deck.
Note
If acquired through Till Death Do You Part before acquiring it through A Matter of Life and Death, you may acquire both Bruxa cards and have 2 in your Gwent Monster deck.
