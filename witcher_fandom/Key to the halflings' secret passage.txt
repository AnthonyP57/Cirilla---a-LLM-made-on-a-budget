Key to the halflings' secret passage
Key to the halflings' secret passage is a key and a quest item in The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt. During the main quest A Poet Under Pressure, after tracking Dandelion and the witch hunter to the Isolated Hut, Geralt will receive this key from a dwarf standing outside the hut with two halflings. It is used to unlock the trap door behind the hut.
