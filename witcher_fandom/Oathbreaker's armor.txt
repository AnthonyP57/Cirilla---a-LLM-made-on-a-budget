Oathbreaker's armor
Oathbreaker's armor is chest armor found in The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings, and The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt.
The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings
Oathbreaker's armor is part of the Oathbreaker's Outfit. It is only available in Dark Mode.
The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt
Oathbreaker armor is a medium armor in The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt, which can be found as random loot.
