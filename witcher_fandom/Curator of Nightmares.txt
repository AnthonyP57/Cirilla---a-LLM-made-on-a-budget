Curator of Nightmares
Curator of Nightmares is an achievement in the Hearts of Stone expansion and is achieved by recreating all of Iris' memories during the main quest Scenes From a Marriage.
There are a total of 8 memories, with 7 of them necessary and part of the quest, while 1 is missable. These include:
courtyard gazebo (missable)
the fountain
the dinner table
the bedroom
the painting studio
Olgierd's study
the estate's cellar
the dining room
