Greater Glyph of Reinforcement
Greater Glyph of Reinforcement is a glyph added on with the Hearts of Stone expansion.
It adds +100% reduction in item durability loss and is needed to make the Eruption, Levity and Retribution glyphwords.
It can only be crafted or purchased from the runewright.
Crafting Requirements 
Min. Required Level: Master armorer/blacksmith
