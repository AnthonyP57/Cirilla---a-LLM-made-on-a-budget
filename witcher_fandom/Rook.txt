Rook
Rook is a potion in The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings.
Journal entry
Rook is especially popular among witchers who believe that killing monsters quickly is their best defense. This potion increases muscular power, and thus damage dealt to foes in close combat. Unlike Thunderbolt, it has no side effects.
Rook can alleviate the side effects of White Raffard's Decoction, Gadwall and Tiara.
Potion causes minor intoxication when imbibed.
The potion is recommended for witchers who prefer to use their swords in fights and employ Signs sporadically. Rook is especially helpful against resilient and heavily armored opponents.
