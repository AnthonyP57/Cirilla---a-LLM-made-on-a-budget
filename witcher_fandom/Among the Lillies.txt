Among the Lillies
Among the Lillies is a battle in Thronebreaker: The Witcher Tales. After the battle, a treasure map and note can be looted.
Entry
Lyrian fisherman claimed that vile monsters had emerged from beneath the water lillies of the Yavina's shallow shore waters. They claimed the horrid beasts had large, fish-like eyes and a scaly hide, speckled with green bristles...
