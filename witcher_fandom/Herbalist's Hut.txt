Herbalist's Hut
Herbalist's Hut, found northeast of Oxenfurt, is home to the halfling herbalist, Otto Bamber.
Map description
Home to a halfling herbalist who is a passionate devotee of innovative gardening methods and experimental herbal medecine.
Associated quests
Without a Trace 
Open Sesame! 
Notes
In the basement you can find a full set of New Moon armor, on the shelf next to the paintings.
