The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt
Unlike previous games, Aether is not simply part of other alchemical ingredients. It is needed to craft the following items:
Blue armor dye 
Superior Cat
Superior Dragon's Dream
Greater blue mutagen 
Superior ogroid oil
Superior Samum
In the base game it must be crafted, but in the Blood and Wine expansion it can also be purchased from alchemists:
Merchant at the Perfumery
Wine Merchant at Chuchote Cave, after completing his quest
Wine Merchant at Dun Tynne Hillside, after completing his quest
