Diagram: Dazhbog runestone
Diagram: Dazhbog runestone is a crafting diagram in The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt that is needed to craft Dazhbog runestones.
It can be purchased from the following merchants:
All blacksmiths in Novigrad, Oxenfurt, Skellige and Toussaint, except the one at Ardaiso Quarry
All alchemists except Jonna in Rannvaig and the Freya Priestess on Hindarsfjall
Merchant at the Isolated Hut on Hindarsfjall
Scoia'tael merchant in the forest south of Novigrad
Runewright at Upper Mill 
Ducal Camerlengo in Beauclair
